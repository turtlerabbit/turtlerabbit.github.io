|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|

|ul|List|-|- List|
|ol|Ordered list|1.|1. Ordered list|
|table|Table|||
|pre|Pre|```js console.log()```|```js console.log()```|
|code|Code|`console.log()`|`console.log()`|
|inlineCode|InlineCode|`inlineCode`|`inlineCode`|
|em|Emphasis|`_emphasis_`|_emphasis_|
|strong|Strong|`**strong**`|**strong**|
|delete|Delete|`~~strikethrough~~`|~~strikethrough~~|
|hr|Thematic break|---- or ***||
|a|Link|`<https://mdxjs.com> or [MDX](https://mdxjs.com)`|<https://mdxjs.com> or [MDX](https://mdxjs.com)|
